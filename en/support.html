Frequently asked questions

These are the support questions most users ask first.

How accurate are the calorie estimates?

Food AI provides calorie and nutrition estimates based on photos, typed input, or nutrition labels. Actual values may vary depending on ingredients, portions, brands, and cooking methods.

Can I log food manually?

Yes. In addition to photo analysis, you can search by food name or create records manually.

Can Food AI analyze nutrition labels?

Yes. The nutrition label feature helps you read calories, protein, fat, and carbohydrate data more clearly.

Does Food AI provide medical advice?

No. Food AI provides general meal tracking, calorie estimates, and health management references only. It does not provide diagnosis, treatment, or professional medical advice.

How do I cancel a subscription?

You can cancel through your Apple ID subscription settings on iPhone or iPad. Exact steps may vary by iOS version.

Who handles payments and refunds?

Subscription payments are handled by Apple App Store. Refund requests must be submitted through Apple’s official process.

Can I request deletion of my data?

Yes. If you want to request access, deletion, or correction of your data, email foodie.eye.2025@gmail.com and describe what you need.
