How we use data

All data use is tied to feature delivery, quality improvement, and issue handling.

  • Provide food recognition, calorie estimates, and nutrition analysis
  • Manage meal, hydration, and weight records
  • Provide AI chat and personalized meal suggestions
  • Generate 7-day meal plans
  • Improve app quality, accuracy, and user experience
  • Handle support requests and issue reports
  • Protect the service against abuse or abnormal activity

AI and third-party services

Some MiraMeal features rely on third-party AI, cloud processing, storage, analytics, or payment services.

When you use photo analysis, nutrition label analysis, or AI chat, your text or images may be sent to relevant services for processing. We make reasonable efforts to work with trusted providers and expect them to follow applicable privacy and security standards.

Subscriptions and payments

Payments are handled by Apple App Store. We do not directly store your full payment credentials.

MiraMeal subscriptions and payments are processed through Apple App Store. We do not directly collect or store your full card number, security code, or Apple payment credentials.

Storage and security

Data is kept only as long as necessary and protected with reasonable technical and administrative measures.

We retain data only for the period needed to operate the service, improve features, comply with legal obligations, or resolve disputes. When data is no longer needed, we reasonably delete, anonymize, or stop using it.

No network transmission or electronic storage method can be guaranteed to be completely secure, but we apply reasonable safeguards.

Your rights

Depending on applicable law, you may request access, correction, deletion, or limitation of certain data processing.

  • Request access to personal data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of specific information
  • Request limitation of certain processing
  • Cancel subscriptions or stop using the service

Children and international transfers

MiraMeal is not designed specifically for children. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children below the legal age threshold in relevant jurisdictions. Depending on infrastructure and service providers, your data may be processed or stored outside your local region, and we apply reasonable protections under applicable law.
